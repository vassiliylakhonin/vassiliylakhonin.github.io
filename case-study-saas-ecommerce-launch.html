Case Study: SaaS / E-Commerce Platform Launch Delivery Control

Context

Consultant assignment (Oct 2021 - May 2022) as Product Launch Coordinator for a SaaS / E-commerce platform rollout. Scope included cross-functional launch coordination, sprint testing cycles, performance tracking, and vendor coordination.

Problem

The launch required synchronized execution across product, testing, and delivery stakeholders while keeping sprint velocity and rollout quality on track. Without disciplined cadence and KPI visibility, timeline slip and post-launch quality risk increase quickly.

Actions

  • Set and maintained 2-week sprint testing cycles aligned to launch milestones.
  • Coordinated vendor and internal teams against a shared action tracker and dependency view.
  • Implemented KPI-based rollout monitoring to flag blockers early and support go/no-go decisions.
  • Standardized status reporting to keep stakeholders aligned on risks, progress, and priority actions.

Results

  • Enabled structured rollout execution across iterative testing cycles through launch phase.
  • Improved cross-team transparency on dependencies and delivery risk.
  • Provided PMO-style control layer for sprint cadence, issue escalation, and stakeholder reporting.

PMO / Compliance relevance

This case demonstrates transferable private-sector capability in program operations: cadence governance, KPI-based performance control, cross-team/vendor coordination, and disciplined execution under timeline pressure.
