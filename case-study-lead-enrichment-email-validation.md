# Case Study: Lead Enrichment + Email Validation (claw0x)

## What it solves
Outbound and CRM workflows lose performance when contact quality is unknown.

## What I built
A monetizable claw0x skill/API that returns structured lead enrichment output, email quality status, confidence, lead score, fit reasons, and risk flags.

## Why it matters
Moves teams from raw lead data to action-ready prioritization in one call.
