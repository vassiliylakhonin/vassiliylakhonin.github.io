# Interview Story Bank (STAR+R)

## Story 1 — Multi-country reporting turnaround
- **Situation:** Multi-country reporting quality was inconsistent across partners and deadlines.
- **Task:** Stabilize reporting cadence and improve submission reliability.
- **Action:** Introduced standard templates, review checkpoints, and weekly risk tracking with partner focal points.
- **Result:** 100% on-time reporting across 8 consecutive quarters; partner delays reduced by 40%.
- **Reflection:** Standardized QA gates plus light-touch governance scales better than ad-hoc follow-ups.

## Story 2 — Audit readiness system
- **Situation:** Documentation and evidence quality needed to be consistently audit-ready.
- **Task:** Build a practical audit-readiness workflow across teams.
- **Action:** Defined evidence taxonomy, ownership map, and pre-audit controls with escalation paths.
- **Result:** Supported 2 external audits with zero findings.
- **Reflection:** Early evidence discipline reduces audit stress and rework.

## Story 3 — KPI governance across countries
- **Situation:** KPI interpretation varied across HQ and country teams.
- **Task:** Align KPI definitions and reporting governance.
- **Action:** Set shared KPI dictionary, governance cadence, and exception handling protocol.
- **Result:** Reliable cross-country KPI tracking across HQ + 4 country offices + 15+ implementing partners.
- **Reflection:** Consistent definitions are as important as data collection tools.

## Story 4 — AI-enabled reporting workflow
- **Situation:** Reporting cycles were slow and manually intensive.
- **Task:** Use AI safely without sacrificing quality.
- **Action:** Added AI-assisted drafting with human QA checkpoints and evidence validation.
- **Result:** Report prep time reduced by 25% while maintaining quality controls.
- **Reflection:** Human-in-the-loop design is key for compliance-critical outputs.

## Story 5 — Product launch operations
- **Situation:** SaaS/e-commerce launch required cross-functional delivery control.
- **Task:** Coordinate timeline, vendor deliverables, and testing cycles.
- **Action:** Ran 2-week sprint cadence, risk log, and execution tracker.
- **Result:** 25+ deliverables delivered on time over 24 months in prior PM role.
- **Reflection:** Rhythm + visibility + accountability drives predictable execution.
