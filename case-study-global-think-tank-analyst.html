Case Study: Global Think Tank Analyst

Context

I rebuilt this skill as a policy-risk memo architect for teams that need decision-ready geopolitical analysis, not narrative-heavy commentary. The production version is published on ClawHub.

Problem

Most AI outputs in policy domains are readable but operationally weak: unclear decision framing, blurred fact vs judgment boundaries, and generic recommendations with low actionability.

Actions

  • Reframed the purpose around decision support, not think-tank style mimicry.
  • Added strict intake fields: question, audience, geography, horizon, actors, domain, depth, evidence mode.
  • Enforced evidence discipline across facts, assumptions, judgments, scenarios, and unknowns.
  • Implemented a 9-step analysis sequence from decision framing to bounded conclusion.
  • Added mode-based outputs: Quick Brief, Standard Memo, Scenario Brief, and Red-Team Challenge.
  • Added confidence protocol (Low/Moderate/High) tied to evidence quality and ambiguity.
  • Added recommendation rules requiring concrete triggers and trade-offs.

What it does now

  • Produces decision-ready policy and geopolitical memos.
  • Surfaces uncertainty explicitly without collapsing into vague caveats.
  • Compares competing interpretations when ambiguity is material.
  • Outputs options with practical costs, downsides, and implementation friction.
  • Defines indicators to watch and trigger conditions for posture change.

Current outputs

  • Executive takeaway.
  • Decision context.
  • Known facts and evidence limits.
  • Actors and incentives.
  • Main assessment.
  • Risks and trade-offs.
  • Options.
  • Indicators to watch.
  • Confidence and key unknowns.

Who it is for

  • Policy analysts and geopolitical researchers.
  • Strategy, risk, and foresight teams.
  • Corporate intelligence functions.
  • Advisory teams producing decision memos under uncertainty.

Why this version is better

It improves decision quality by enforcing analytical discipline, transparent uncertainty, and actionable option design instead of generic summary output.

Tech stack

  • OpenClaw runtime and orchestration.
  • ClawHub distribution.
  • Instruction-first mode architecture with evidence and confidence guardrails.

Relevance for future roles

Demonstrates practical AI system design for policy and strategic risk workflows where traceability, judgment quality, and explicit limits matter.
