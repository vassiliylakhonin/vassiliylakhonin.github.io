# Case Study: Agent Change Safety

## What it solves
Many incidents happen during configuration and integration changes, not during normal operation.

## What I built
A production change protocol skill with preflight checks, risk scoring, rollback planning, HITL gates, and post-change validation checkpoints.

## Why it matters
Adds operational safety guardrails to agent-led system changes.
